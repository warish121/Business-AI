Business AI Agent

This project represents a sophisticated AI-powered Business Intelligence Agent built using Google Agent Development kit that revolutionizes how businesses analyze product performance, pricing strategies, and demand forecasting. The system combines artificial intelligence with robust data analytics to provide comprehensive insights into product lifecycle management.
It gives your product a great boost that will increase your demand of that product.

** Google ADK
** LlmAgnet
** Python

<h1>** Custom Tool Function**</h1>

<h3>**get_product_price_history**</h3>
This function serves as the system 's pricing oracle, providing deep historical analysis of product pricing strategies. It doesn't merely track price change; it perform sophisticated trend analysis by comparing recent price movements against historical patterns. The engine calculates comprehensive statistics including:

Price volatility metrics to assess market stability.

Trend identification

Comparative analysis between current and historical price point

<h3>**get_sales_data**</h3>

This component function as a virtual sales analyst, offering both granular and holistic views of business performance.

Product-specific analysis for detailed performance tracking
Portfolio-wide assessment for strategic business planning
The analytics engine calculate key performance indicator including total unit sold, revenue generation, average daily sold, and market share distribution.
The system also provide last 7 days granular data for immediate tactical decision making.

<h3>**calculate_demand_trend**</h3>
This represent the most advanced component of the system, employing predictive analytics to anticipate market movement. The function combines multiple analytical approaches:

Growth rate calculation based on weekly performance
-Demand classification system categorizing products as HIGH_GROWTH, GROWING, STABLE, DECLINING, LOW_DEMAND.
Seasonality analysis to identify recurring patterns.
